Welcome to Wild Rescues. Our mission to rescue, rehabilitate, and release wildlife back into their natural environment. We are nonprofit organization dedicated to helping injured wildlife.

Our staff provides around-the-clock care for wildlife in need. We also provide long-term care for wildlife that cannot be released back into their natural environment.

Will you partner with us? We need caring volunteers to help care for our animals. You can also donate, become a memeber, or become a sponsor. Contact us today.
